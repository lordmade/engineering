Breaking Barriers.

Building the future of South African engineering — one powerful woman at a time.

Ellen Shabangu

Founder & Managing Director

A visionary leader who refused to accept limits. From community development projects in Mapela to pioneering a 100% black female-owned engineering company serving platinum mines across South Africa — Ellen has proven that excellence knows no gender.

With unwavering integrity and a passion for uplifting others, Ellen built Pitja Engineering from the ground up in 2017. Today, we deliver unbroken OEM-quality service to some of the biggest names in mining — all while maintaining Level 1 B-BBEE status and empowering the next generation of women in engineering.
