Our Purpose

Pitja Engineering (PTY) Ltd is a 100% female black-owned company established in 2017, with a purpose to explore opportunities in the male-dominated engineering field. We employ the highest level of integrity, values, morals, and standards.

The director of Pitja Engineering (PTY) Ltd, Ellen Shabangu, has acquired many leadership skills through programs and roles in a number of community initiatives. This experience provides a significant advantage to the company, as her knowledge and work ethic will add value in regard to leadership and experience.

Ellen is a woman who believes greatly in community upliftment. She has engaged in numerous community activities, such as crime awareness programs in Mapela and the Nkone community. She is renowned for her roles in bringing local women into agricultural projects and venturing into cosmetic and kitchen businesses historically. Refusing to be limited, she has broken into the male-dominated engineering world. Under Ellen's leadership, Pitja now boasts over one year of unbroken service to one of the reputable mines.
