About Us

Pitja Engineering (PTY) Ltd is a 100% female black-owned company established in 2017, with a purpose to explore opportunities in the male-dominated engineering field. We employ the highest level of integrity, values, morals, and standards.

Director Ellen Shabangu has acquired many leadership skills through community roles, adding value to the company in leadership and experience. Ellen believes in community upliftment, engaging in activities like crime awareness in Mapela and Nkone, bringing local women into agricultural projects, and venturing into cosmetic and kitchen businesses. Through her leadership, Pitja now has over 1 year of unbroken service to reputable mines.
